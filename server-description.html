About IslesRP

Welcome to Isles Server – a welcoming, chill community built on inclusivity and respect. Isles is very focused on listening to user feedback and continuously evolving to create a better roleplay experience for everyone.

The server stays true to GTA V’s lore by keeping vehicles authentic to real GTA car manufacturers. Whether you’re cruising in a classic muscle car or a sleek modern ride, every detail is carefully maintained for realism.

There's always something to do on IslesRP — from engaging in jobs, crime, and PD/EMS roleplay to exploring endless opportunities for adventure. The friendly, helpful community ensures that no matter your role or experience, you’ll feel right at home.
