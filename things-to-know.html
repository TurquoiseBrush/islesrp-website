Pure Mode

The server uses Pure Mode, follow these steps to enable it:

  1. Right click your FiveM app
  2. Go to properties
  3. On the target line, add -pure_1 after the .exe
  4. Click apply
Clearing FiveM Cache

Follow these steps to clear your FiveM cache on Windows:

  1. Open the Windows search bar, type %localappdata%, and press Enter.
  2. Locate and open the FiveM folder.
  3. Open the FiveM Application Data folder.
  4. Within this folder, open the Data folder.
  5. Hold Ctrl and click the folders: Cache, Server-Cache, and Server-Cache-Priv.
  6. Right-click the selected folders and choose Delete, or press the Del key.
